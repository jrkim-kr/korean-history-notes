# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Korean naturalization interview candidates who study on mobile and desktop, often switching between Korean and Chinese while reviewing question cards.

## Product Purpose

Provide a focused bilingual study tool for the 2026 Korean naturalization interview question set. Success means users can quickly filter, favorite, review, and share the questions they need to practice.

## Brand Personality

Focused, practical, calm. The interface should feel like a study tool, not a marketing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative landing-page patterns, oversized promotional sections, distracting animation, and layouts that waste mobile screen space.

## Design Principles

- Keep the card and current study controls immediately usable on small screens.
- Make counts, filters, and favorites visibly consistent with the active study state.
- Prefer predictable product UI patterns over decorative novelty.
- Preserve bilingual clarity without crowding Korean and Chinese labels.
- Optimize for repeated study sessions, not one-time browsing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard operation for quiz navigation, maintain visible focus states, and keep touch targets large enough for mobile use.
